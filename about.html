Our story

LuxeLight began with a single bulb moment in a small studio in the Peak District. Our founder spent nights sketching silhouettes of light that could transform quiet rooms into calm sanctuaries. Friends gathered to prototype shades in brass and glass, and soon the studio glow spread to homes across the country.

We design with a simple promise: honest materials, considered engineering, and finishes that age beautifully. Every piece is inspected in our workshop and packed with care. When your parcel arrives, we want you to feel the same quiet excitement we do when a new lamp is switched on for the first time.
